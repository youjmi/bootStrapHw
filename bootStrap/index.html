About Me

Hafa Adai and Hello!

Thank you for visiting my website. My name is Jeezy and I was born and raised on the beautiful island of Guam. I currently have a Bachelor's in Hospitality and Tourism Management and a minor in Economics after spending some time in Massachusetts for college. Shortly after college, I moved to Washington D.C for my job. Although I am still so far away from my family, I rely on websites such as this one in hopes that it will lessen homesickness.
For those who are curious about what Guam has to offer, feel free to take a look at my website to learn more about me and my island!
